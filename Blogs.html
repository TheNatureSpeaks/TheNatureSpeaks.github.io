You Hold The Nature's Future

By: Michelle D. Pangilinan

    Nature is the physical world and everything in it (such as plants, animals, mountains, oceans, stars, etc.) that is not made by people (Merriam, 14th Century) It serves as a God's given gift that if we will not maintain, it can take many people's lives. Nature for me is my source of relaxation. I can find my inner peace through the sound of wavering waves at the shore. Also, the breathtaking views on top of the mountains or the oddly satisfying natural tourist's spots. 

    Nature is also the home for our animals and plants. It's their natural habitat that if we will not protect at all costs, I can say its our loss too. Nature is our number one supplier of goods. Without nature, we can't build anything that stands today. 

    Nature can speak. It's speaking in various ways, such as natural calamities, good harvest, good weather, and etc. Natural calamities are ways of nature to take revenge from us, people. Just in instant, it can take millions of lives. 

    Just like littering, you can do it instantly. Good weather and good harvest are example of nature's gratitude. If you're good, she's good. For me, nature is our mother. She doesn't only gives us the bad to learn, also the good, to earn. 

"Every one of us holds our nature's future. Be the one to start the movement!"

DEFORESTATION

By: Denise Nicole Danganan

    Our team ought to help in a little but useful way. According to Cambridge dictionary deforestation define as "the cutting down of trees in a large area, or the destruction of forests by people". As stated in the dictionary people are the main cause of this destruction. 


It also means that deforestation depends on the people if they will continue their wrongdoings or be a better person that will make the environment a better place.

    By making a website our group's mission is to open the eyes of the community and to educate them about the cause and effect of deforestation.Trees are truly useful in numerous ways. 

    Trees serves as a shed and protection from the sun. If there is a calamity the tree sips the flood to reduce it. Just imagine if there were no more trees, our environment will be empty and blank. 

    The goal our group want to achieve is to make the people as one in saving the environment. And our Vision is to encourage people to plant trees. With the benefits and advantages the trees are giving we need to take good care of them and most importantly our environment.

"In achieving one ambition with the people in the community we can attain our group's Mission, Vision and Goals."

An Unexpected Casualty

By: Sherenade Anne Gonzales

Last September 2009, one of the strongest typhoon known in the Philippines as Tropical Storm Ondoy, hits the islands resulting to 58,691,595,000.00 Philippine peso amount of damages and 747 casualties. Though this type of calamity cannot be stop but we can at least, do something so that its effect won’t be that big. Let me share you a story, about how a family of seven has been affected by this type of calamity even though they live in a altitudinous rural community. 

    Tuesday that a kid aged 9 will never forget. September 24, almost ten in the evening. Her grandmother woke her up because they are in dire need of going to a higher place to spend the night with. In her confusion, why? They live in one of the highest place in Bulacan, but because she’s still a kid she is in the state of confusion. That time, she may not understand everything but she know something is wrong and the people can do something about it.

    She carried her little brother, aged six then minutes later, she ran with her grandmother aged forty-six and her grandfather, forty-five. Using their tricycle as their only means of travelling faster than walking, her grandfather hurriedly started the motor, and on the other side, her mother and two uncles are running with some things and equipments with them, some clothes and some appliances that they can save. They never really expected this type of calamity to hit on them. 

    Fortunately, they were saved. But still, the mind of a nine-year old girl are asking, “Why did all of this happen even if we are already in an altitudinous community?” For several, adults won’t accept the truth. That we, people, are doing this to ourselves. 

"Cutting trees, throwing garbages improperly, cutting trees, deforestation and such. We can always do something about it — plant trees now."
